About

I’m Aiden, a web developer from the Southern United States. I have a passion for the passionate. I love helping small businesses and startups translate the core of their brand into a unique web design. I’m also a fan of flavored lattes, PC gaming, discovering new music, and podcasts!

Process

01. Interview/Research

This is where I learn all about who you are and what your brand stands for! We’ll discuss your goals for the site, your customer base, and your likes/dislikes. During this phase, I will also research competitors to make sure you stand out in the crowd.

02. Sitemap & Wireframe

I create a sitemap that will determine the website’s navigation and hierarchy. Then, I create a wireframe of the potiential layout of your site.

03. Design

After the wireframe is approved, I will begin filling it in with the design! I may use other tools, such as After Effects & Blender at this point to create custom artwork and animations.

04. Development

The final step is to bring the website to life! Depending on your needs, this can be done in a website builder, such as WIX, or with code.
