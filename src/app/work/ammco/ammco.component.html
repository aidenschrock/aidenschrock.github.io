The African Marine Mammal Conservation Organization(AMMCO) is a non-profit dedicated to reversing the countdown on animal extinction.

Tech Stack: Flutter, Dart, Material Design

My role with AMMCO revolves around their Siren-Turtles project. Their apps allow scientists, researchers, and fishermen to add observations of turtles to the Siren database. This data aids the conservation efforts of endangered turtle species.

When I joined the team, they were preparing to migrate their mobile app. It was originally built in Ionic and users were reporting quite a few bugs. The engineering team chose to use Flutter to build the new mobile app for the following reasons:

  • Performance
  • Ability to build for both Android and IOS simultaneously
  • It's new and exciting

Due to the nature of volunteer work, I put a lot of emphasis on organizing and writing descriptive code. I want to ensure that any new engineers will be able to quickly grasp the codebase.

This role has allowed me to spearhead the development of a mobile app and collaborate with an international team of designers and engineers!
