Recolude is a a startup providing 3D spatial recording utilities for XR, research, gaming, and mocap projects.

Tech Stack: Angular 9, TypeScript, SCSS, Threejs, Apache Echarts, Go, AWS Cognito/Amplify

Check out this embeded demo!

When I first started doing work for Recolude, they were still in the process of developing their brand's image. Their logo and website felt dull in comparison to their bleeding-edge services. Keeping in mind both their product and audience, I redesigned their logo to be sharper, more legible, and more colorful.

With the new logo completed, it was time to update Recolude's web app and website. The team requested custom components in their app's login/signup flow to replace their default AWS Amplify components. For this, I worked with Angular, SASS, and AWS Cognito. I also created a custom illustration of their mascot to greet users. The mascot image is a placeholder, as soon they will have one of their 3D spatial recordings embedded there.

Making large amounts of information user friendly is the biggest challenge I face when developing for Recolude. Each recording can hold an unlimited amount of metadata! I redesigned the webplayer's sidebar to make exploring this data more intuitive.

Recolude has expanded its analytics features to include analytic dashboards that utilize a custom API. My involvement included choosing the charting library, designing the dashboard, and initiating the frontend development. Below is an example image.

For the Recolude website, I used Wix's Editor X. Their original website was in WordPress, a website builder notorious for being hard to manage. Though WIX has its flaws, Recolude can now easily update their site without having to start from scratch or pay more than they should! My design choices were based on their target demographic: Indie 3D game developers. I used strong, bright colors with a dark theme and layered components to pop.
