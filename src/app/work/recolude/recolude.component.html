Recolude is a a startup providing 3D spatial recording utilities for XR, research, gaming, and mocap projects.

When I first started doing work for Recolude, they were still in the process of developing their brand's image. Their logo and website felt dull in comparison to their bleeding edge services. Keeping in mind both their product and audience, I redesigned their logo to be sharper, more legible, and more colorful.

With the new logo completed, it was time to update Recolude's web app and website. The team requested custom components in their app's login/signup flow to replace their default AWS Amplify components. For this, I worked with Angular, SASS, and AWS Cognito. I also created a custom illustration of their mascot to greet users. The mascot image is made to be a placeholder, as soon they will have one of their 3D spatial recordings embedded there.

For the Recolude website, I used Wix's Editor X. Their original website was in wordpress, a website builder notorious for being hard to manage. Though WIX has it's flaws, Recolude can now easily update their site without having to start from scartch or pay more than they should! My design choices were based on their target demographic: Indie 3D game developers. I used strong, bright colors with a dark theme and layered components to pop.
